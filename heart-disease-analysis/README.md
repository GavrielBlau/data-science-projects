# Heart Disease Risk Analysis

This project analyzes a heart attack prediction dataset to explore relationships between medical variables and the probability of heart disease. The analysis includes data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, descriptive statistics, and visualization of key health indicators such as cholesterol levels, blood pressure, heart rate, and age.

Statistical techniques were used to examine distributions, detect outliers, and understand how different variables may relate to heart attack risk. The project demonstrates the use of Python, pandas, and visualization tools to analyze medical datasets and identify meaningful patterns in health data.
